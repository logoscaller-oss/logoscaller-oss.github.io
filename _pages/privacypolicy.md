# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LogosCaller (想啥呢) collects, uses, and protects your information.

<br>

## 1.0 Information We Collect
We collect information that helps us provide the best community experience for connecting problems and solutions.

### 1.1 Information you provide to us 
- **Account Information**: When you register, we collect basic details like your username and email address.
- **Content**: We store the "Roasts", "Tips", "Ideas", and comments you publish on the platform.

<br>

## 2.0 How we use your information
- **Service Provision**: To display your content and facilitate community interactions.
- **AI Features**: To process your inputs for AI-assisted brainstorming and validation (data is not used to train public models without consent).
- **Communication**: To send you notifications about activity on your posts or platform updates.

<br>

## 3.0 Data Security
We implement standard security measures to protect your personal information and content from unauthorized access.

<br>

## 4.0 Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sparkpool.com.
